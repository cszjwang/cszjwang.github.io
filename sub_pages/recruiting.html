Recruiting:  

Dr. Zhi-Jie Wang is looking for excellent students to do exciting research. Smart and hardworking master students with good English/math and strong coding ability are especially welcomed.

Recently, his research group mainly focuses on scientific problems related to

  • big data,

  • artificial intelligence,

  • data mining, and

  • distributed systems.

The minimum requirements for possible candidates are:

  1. you have passion for research;

  2. you can read papers and understand the main ideas therein; and

  3. you can implement your research ideas using at least one of programming languages.

Please feel free to email him if you are interested!

[Remark: The second and third year undergraduates @ CQU are also warmly welcomed to join his research group. Usually, at the initial stage, undergraduate students will work with him and/or a senior graduate student on a research project. You may learn to do research and get published earlier than your peers. It is worthy noting that undergraduates who worked with him for more than 1 year will be given special recommendation when they graduate.]
