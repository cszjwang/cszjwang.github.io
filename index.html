Biography
 
​​​Dr. Wang received the Ph.D in computer science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, and did a postdoc at the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Kowloon, Hong Kong. He is currently an Associate Professor at the College of Computer Science, Chongqing University (CQU), Chongqing, China. Before joining CQU, he worked at the School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU), Guangzhou, China. He is/was a Committee Member of CCF YOCSEF Guangzhou, a Committee Member of Guangdong Big Data Professional Committee, and a Communication Committee Member of CCF Technical Committee of Database. He is a Member of CCF, IEEE, and ACM.
