Biography

​​​He was born in a small village of Hunan Province, China. He is currently an Associate Professor at the College of Computer Science, Chongqing University (CQU), Chongqing, China. He received the Ph.D in computer science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, and did a postdoc at the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Kowloon, Hong Kong.  Before joining CQU, he was a Research Associate Professor at the School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU), Guangzhou, China. He is a Member of CCF, IEEE, and ACM. Also, he is/was a Committee Member of CCF YOCSEF Guangzhou, a Committee Member of Guangdong Big Data Professional Committee, and a Communication Committee Member of CCF Technical Committee of Database.

Research Students

Recruiting: He is looking for excellent students to do exciting research. Smart and hardworking students with good English/math and strong coding ability are especially welcomed. Recently, his research group mainly focuses on scientific problems related to big data, artificial intelligence, data mining and distributed systems. The minimum requirements for possible candidates are: (i) you have passion for research; (ii) you can read papers and understand the main ideas therein; and (iii) you can implement your research ideas using at least one of programming languages. Please feel free to email him if you are interested!

[The second and third year undergraduates @ CQU are also warmly welcomed to join his research group. Usually, at the initial stage, undergraduate students will work with him and/or a senior graduate student on a research project. You may learn to do research and get published earlier than your peers. It is worthy noting that undergraduates who worked with him for more than 1 year will be given special recommendation when they graduate.]
