Dr. Wang is looking for excellent master and PhD students to do exciting research. Smart and hardworking students with good English/math and strong coding ability are especially welcomed. Recently, his research group mainly focuses on scientific problems related to artificial intelligence, data mining, big data, and machine learning. The minimum requirements for possible candidates are:

  1. you have passion for research;

  2. you can read papers and understand the main ideas therein; and

  3. you can implement your research ideas using at least one of programming languages.

Remark 1: The undergraduates @ CQU are also warmly welcomed to join his research group. Usually, at the initial stage, undergraduate students will work with him and/or a senior graduate student on a research project. You may learn to do research and get published earlier than your peers. It is worthy noting that undergraduates who worked with him for more than 1 year will be given special recommendation when they graduate.

Remark 2: He is also looking for Postdocs. The minimum requirements for possible candidates are:

  1. you have published one CCF Rank B paper or above;  

  2. you are willing to cooperate with his undergraduate/master/PhD students.

Please feel free to email your CV to him if you are interested!
