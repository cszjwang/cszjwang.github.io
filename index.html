Biography

​​​He was born in a small village of Hunan Province, China. He is currently a research associate professor at the School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU), Guangzhou, China. He received the Ph.D in computer science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. Before joining SYSU, he was a postdoctoral research fellow at the Department of Computing, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Kowloon, Hong Kong. He is a Member of CCF, IEEE, and ACM. Also, he is a Committee Member of CCF YOCSEF Guangzhou, a Committee Member of Guangdong Big Data Professional Committee, and a Communication Committee Member of CCF Technical Committee of Database.

Research Students

The minimum requirements for possible candidates are: (i) you have passion for research; (ii) you can read papers and understand the main ideas therein; and (iii) you can implement your research ideas using at least one of programming languages.
