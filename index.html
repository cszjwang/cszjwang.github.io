Biography

​​​He was born in a small village in Shimen County, Hunan Province, China, on December 14, 1980. He is currently a research associate professor at the School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-Sen University (SYSU), Guangzhou, China. He received the Ph.D in computer science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, in 2015. Before joining SYSU in 2017, he was a postdoctoral research fellow at the Department of Computing, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Kowloon, Hong Kong.

Professional Services

He was the invited reviewer, program committee member, and/or external reviewer for a lot of journals/conferences such as TODS, TKDE, Geoinformatica, VLDB, ICDE, MDM, NDBC, etc.
